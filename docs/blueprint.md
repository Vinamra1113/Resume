# **App Name**: Vinamra Jain - Portfolio

## Core Features:

- Profile Information: Display Vinamra Jain’s key details, including profile photo, full name, location, contact information, and quick-access links to LinkedIn, GitHub, Linktree, and other professional profiles.
- Career Objective: Showcase Vinamra’s career objective, clearly highlighting his professional focus, core strengths, and long-term career goals in analytics, business intelligence, Salesforce, and data-driven roles.
- Education Showcase: Present Vinamra’s educational background with degree details, institutions, CGPA/percentages, and academic timelines in a structured and easy-to-read format.
- Technical Skills: List technical skills in categorized sections such as Data Analytics & BI, Programming & Machine Learning, Platforms & Tools, and Financial Analysis to clearly demonstrate Vinamra’s technical expertise.
- Interactive Project Portfolio: Display Vinamra’s projects with concise descriptions, defined roles, technology stacks, project timelines, and direct links for live demos, dashboards, or case studies, enabling interactive exploration.
- Training and Certifications: Showcase relevant training programs and certifications with issuing organizations, completion dates, credential details, and direct verification links to enhance professional credibility.
- Achievements and Recognition: Highlight key achievements, awards, and recognitions with brief context and supporting links to showcase academic excellence and professional milestones.
- Professional Experience: Present Vinamra’s work experience with role titles, organizations, duration, and key responsibilities, focusing on real-world impact and industry exposure.
- AI-Powered Content Enhancement: Integrate AI-based tools to generate alternative text for project images, dashboards, and demos, improving accessibility, SEO performance, and inclusive design compliance.
- Resume Download and Sharing: Provide an option to download an ATS-optimized resume (PDF) and share the portfolio easily across job portals and professional networks.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep navy blue (#1A237E) to convey professionalism and trustworthiness.
- Background color: Light gray (#F5F5F5) for a clean and modern backdrop.
- Accent color: Soft lavender (#C5CAE9) to add a touch of creativity and distinction, while remaining subtle.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', sans-serif, for a modern, neutral, and highly readable text.
- Use professional and consistent icons to represent different skills and categories.
- Implement a clean, structured layout with clear sections and visual hierarchy for easy navigation.
- Incorporate subtle animations, like smooth transitions or hover effects, to enhance user engagement without being distracting.
